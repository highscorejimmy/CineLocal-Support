Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2026

CineLocal ("the App") is designed to help you organize and play your personal video collection on your iPhone. Your privacy is fundamental to how the App works. This policy explains what information is processed, where it is stored, and what leaves your device.

Summary

  • We do not collect personal data. There are no accounts, analytics, or advertising trackers.
  • Your videos stay on your device. Imported files are copied into the App's secure sandbox.
  • Metadata lookups are optional and outbound only. The App may query public movie databases to find posters and details.
  • We do not sell or share your information. We have no servers that receive your library or media files.

Information Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following locally on your iPhone:

  • Video files you choose to import
  • Cached poster images
  • Library metadata (titles, years, overviews, genres, ratings, and related details)
  • An optional TMDB API key, if you choose to provide one in Settings
  • App preferences stored via iOS standard local storage (UserDefaults / AppStorage)

This data remains on your device unless you delete it (for example, by removing a movie from your library or uninstalling the App). Uninstalling the App removes its sandboxed data from your device.

Information Sent Over the Internet

When you import a video or refresh metadata, the App may send movie search queries (derived from filenames or titles you already have locally) to third-party services in order to retrieve public movie information and artwork. These requests do not include your video files.

Third-party services the App may contact:

  • The Movie Database (TMDB) — used when you provide an optional TMDB API key in Settings. Requests include search terms and your API key. See TMDB's privacy policy.
  • Apple iTunes Search API — used as a fallback for metadata lookup when no TMDB key is set. Requests include search terms only. See Apple's privacy information.

Poster images retrieved from these services are downloaded and cached locally on your device for offline use.

What We Do Not Collect

CineLocal does not:

  • Require or create user accounts
  • Upload your videos or personal files to any server operated by us
  • Use analytics, crash-reporting, or advertising SDKs that track you across apps
  • Collect your name, email address, phone number, or location
  • Sell, rent, or trade your information

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Because no account or personal data collection is involved, the App is suitable for general audiences subject to the content of videos you import yourself.

Data Security

Your imported media and library data are protected by iOS app sandboxing. Only the App (and your device backups, if enabled) can access this data. We recommend keeping your device passcode or biometric lock enabled.

Your Choices

  • You choose which videos to import
  • You can delete individual movies and their associated files at any time
  • You can remove your TMDB API key from Settings
  • You can use the App offline after initial import and metadata caching

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on our GitHub support repository or visit our Support page.
